How it works

From first message to organized case in three simple steps.

1

Client tells their story

The client types what happened in simple language — via chat on your website, landing page or WhatsApp integration.

2

LegalFlow organizes and scores

The platform transforms the story into a structured case: probable area of law, risk, urgency, value indication and recommended next step.

3

Lawyer takes control

The responsible lawyer sees the case in a clean dashboard, updates the status and the client follows everything in their own portal.
